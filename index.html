About

Chris, a Canadian-born Filipino designer based in Calgary, Alberta, infuses creativity and innovation into every aspect of his work. Armed with a Marketing degree from the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology, he ventured into the realm of Interactive Design, fueling his passion for cutting-edge concepts.

With a diverse background spanning the public sector with the City of Calgary and the dynamic startup scene with Neo Financial, Chris thrives on challenges. At Neo Financial, he seamlessly juggled various roles, from nurturing partnerships to leading creative design endeavors and driving product enhancements.

Outside of the boardroom, Chris finds his rhythm in the dance studio, pushes his limits at the gym, and embraces the thrill of training for his next marathon. His zest for life and relentless pursuit of excellence define him both personally and professionally.
